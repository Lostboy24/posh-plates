The Room

A table set with intention, in the heart of Jalandhar.

Posh Plates was built on a simple idea: a plate should be as considered as the room around it. Every dish leaves the pass with the same quiet precision, whether it's landing at a two-top or the private dining room.

We keep the menu tight, the produce seasonal, and the service unhurried — the kind of dinner you plan an evening around.
